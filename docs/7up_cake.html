7-up Cake

Category: Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cup butter
  • 3 cup sugar
  • 5 eggs
  • 3 cup flour
  • 2 tablespoon lemon extract
  • 3/4 cup 7-up

Steps:

  1. Cream together butter and sugar for 20 minutes.
  2. Add eggs, one at a time.
  3. Add flour and lemon extract.
  4. Fold in 7-up.
  5. Pour into a well-greased 12 cup bundt pan.
  6. Bake @ 325Â°F for 1 to 1 1/4 hours.
