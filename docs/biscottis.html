Biscottis

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 cup flour
  • 2 teaspoon double acting baking powder
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 5 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 2 egg
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon grated lemon rind
  • Choose:
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
      • or
    • 1 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1 cup chopped slivered almonds
  • chocolate

Steps:

  1. Combine flour and baking powder and set aside.
  2. Mix sugar and butter until creamy. Add eggs, lemond rind, and extract. Add dry ingredients gradually until well blended. Mix in almonds.
  3. Divided dough in half. Using wet hands, form into 2 x 12 inch logs. Flatten to 1/2 inch thick.
  4. Bake 18 minutes at 350Â°F on ungreased cookie sheet.
  5. Place on cutting board and cut into 1 inch slices. Place slices on sides on cookie sheet.
  6. Bake 12 minutes.
  7. Allow to cool. Dip into melted chocolate.
