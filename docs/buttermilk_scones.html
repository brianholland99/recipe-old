Buttermilk Scones

Ingredients:

  • 4 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3 3/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup plus 2 tablespoon butter
  • Choose:
    • 1 1/2 cup dried fruit
    • 1/2 teaspoon grated orange peel
      • or
    • 1 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  • 1 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • Choose:
    • 2 tablespoon milk
      • or
    • 2 tablespoon cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 3 tablespoon sugar

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 425Â°F.
  2. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Add butter and mix until it resembles coarse cornmeal.
  4. Put into a large bowl and stir in dried fruit and orange peel or chocolate chips.
  5. Add buttermilk and stir with a fork until dough cleans the sides of the bowl.
  6. Gather half dough and roll into a ball on a lightly floured surface.
  7. Roll or pat until about a half inch thick.
  8. Cut with a knife into eight pie shaped pieces.
  9. Repeat with remaining dough.
  10. Place on a lightly greased cookie sheet and brush tops with milk or cream.
  11. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon mixture.
  12. Bake for 10 minutes.
Comment: Strawberries, blueberries, and cherries make good choices for fruit. The scones are moister when slightly under cooked, about 9 minutes.
