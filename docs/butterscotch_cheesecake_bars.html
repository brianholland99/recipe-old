Butterscotch Cheesecake Bars

Ingredients:

  • 2 cup butterscotch chips
  • 1/2 cup margarine
  • 2 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • 1 cup chopped pecans
  • 8 ounces softened cream cheese
  • 14 ounce sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Combine butterscotch chips and margarine in double boiler over hot water. Cook until melted.
  3. Add graham cracker crumbs and pecans; mix well.
  4. Press half the graham cracker mixture into greased 9-by-13 inch baking pan.
  5. Combine cream cheese, condensed milk, egg, and vanilla in bowl; mix well.
  6. Spread over graham cracker layer. Sprinkle with remaining graham cracker crumb mixture.
  7. Bake at 350°F for 20 to 30 minutes or until top is firm.
  8. Cut into 1-by-2-inch bars.
Makes 15
