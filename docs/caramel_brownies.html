Caramel Brownies

Ingredients:

  • Choose:
    • 1 box German chocolate cake mix
      • or
    • 1 box Devil foods cake mix
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 pound caramels

Steps:

  1. Melt the caramels and half the can of condensed milk in a pan on the stove top.
  2. Mix the butter (melted), the cake mix and the rest of the condensed milk.
  3. Spread half the cake mix on the bottom of a greased 9x13 pan.
  4. Bake at 350Â°F for 6-8 minutes.
  5. Bring out of the oven and put the chocolate chips over the mix, then pour the melted caramels on top of this and finish up by crumbling the rest of the cake mixture on the top.
  6. Finish baking for 15-18 minutes.
  7. Let cool completely before cutting or the caramel will ooze out.
