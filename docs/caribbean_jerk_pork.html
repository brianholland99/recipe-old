Caribbean Jerk Pork

Category: Beef and Pork

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoon dark rum (optional)
  • 2 tablespoon honey
  • 2 juice only limes
  • 4 chopped green onions
  • 4 crushed garlic cloves
  • 2 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 small hot chili pepper, seeded & chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground pepper
  • 1 1/2 pound boneless pork tenderloin, trimmed of silverskin

Steps:

  1. Combine all ingredients, except pork, in a medium bowl.
  2. Add pork.
  3. Cover bowl, and marinate at least 4 hours or overnight, turning pork occasionally.
  4. Preheat grill (to HIGH if using gas or electric grill.
  5. Cook 30 to 40 minutes or until meat is cooked through (160Â°F on meat thermometer), turning every 5 minutes.
  6. Set aside for 5 minutes before cutting in to thin, diagonal slices to serve.
Serves 6
Comment: Can be surprisingly spicy. I never use the rum, and I believe it makes fewer than 6 servings.
