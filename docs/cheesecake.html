Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 24 ounce room temperature cream cheese
  • 1 cup room temperature sour cream
  • 1 cup room temperature whipping cream
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon flour
  • 4 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Steps:

  1. Whip cream cheese, sour cream, sugar, and flour until creamy.
  2. Add eggs.
  3. Add whipping cream and vanilla. Beat 2 minutes.
  4. Bake in oven at 300Â°F for 1 hour.
  5. Turn oven off and let stand in oven for 1 more hour.
  6. Refrigerate for at least 5 hours.
