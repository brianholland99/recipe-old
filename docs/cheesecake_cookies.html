Cheesecake Cookies

crust and topping

    Ingredients:

    • 1/3 cup butter
    • 1/3 cup firmly packed brown sugar
    • 1 cup flour
    • 1/2 cup chopped nuts

cheese mixture

    Ingredients:

    • 1/4 cup sugar
    • 8 ounce cream cheese
    • 1 egg
    • 2 tablespoon milk
    • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
    • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla

Steps:

  1. Cream butter and brown sugar.
  2. Add flour and nuts. Mix to make crumb mixture.
  3. Reserve 1 cup for topping.
  4. Press remaining crumb mixture in 8" square pan.
  5. Bake at 350°F for 10 to 15 minutes until lightly brown. Cool.
  6. Blend sugar with cream cheese in a small bowl. Add egg, milk lemon juice and vanilla. (will be kind of watery)
  7. Spread over baked crust.
  8. Sprinkle rest of topping mixture over cheese mixture.
  9. Bake at 350°F for 25 minutes. Cool. Cut into squares.
