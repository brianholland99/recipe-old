Cherry Swirl Coffee Cake

cake

    Ingredients:

    • 4 cup Bisquick baking mix
    • 1/2 cup sugar
    • 1/4 cup melted margarine
    • 1/2 cup milk
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla
    • 1 teaspoon almond extract
    • 3 egg
    • 21 ounce canned cherry, apricot or blueberry pie filling

glaze

    Ingredients:

    • 1 cup powdered sugar
    • 1 tablespoon - 2 tablespoon milk
steps:
  1. Beat powdered sugar and milk until smooth and of desired consistency.

Steps:

  1. Heat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Grease jelly roll pan, 15 1/2x10 1/2x1", or 2 square pans, 9x9x2".
  3. Mix all ingredients except pie filling and glaze; beat vigorously 30 sec.
  4. Spread 2/3 of the batter (about 2 1/2 cups) in jelly roll pan or 1/3 of the batter (about 1 1/4 cups) in each square pan.
  5. Spread pie filling over batter (filling may not cover batter completely).
  6. Drop remaining batter by taplespoon onto the pie filling.
  7. Bake until light brown, 20 to 25 min.
  8. Drizzle with glaze while warm. Serve warm or cool.
Serves 18
