Chewy Oatmeal Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup Butter Flavor Crisco
  • 1 1/4 cup firmly packed light brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 3 cup uncooked quick oats
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 cup raisins
  • 1 cup coarsely chopped walnuts

Steps:

  1. Heat oven to 375°F. Grease baking sheets. Place sheets of foil on countertop for cooling cookies.
  2. Combine Crisco, brown sugar, egg, milk, and vanilla in large bowl. Beat at medium speed of electric mixer until well blended.
  3. Combine oats, flour, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon. Mix into creamed mixture at low speed just until blended.
  4. Stir in raisins and nuts.
  5. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough 2 inches apart onto baking sheet.
  6. Bake one baking sheet at a time at 375°F for 10 to 12 minutes, or until lightly browned.
  7. Cool 2 minutes on baking sheet. Remove cokies to foil to cool completely.
Serves
