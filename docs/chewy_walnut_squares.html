Chewy Walnut Squares

Ingredients:

  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup firmly packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup coarsely chopped walnuts

Steps:

  1. Grease an 8-inch square pan.
  2. Stir together the egg, brown sugar, and vanilla.
  3. Quickly stir in flour, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Add walnuts.
  5. Spread in pan and bake at 350Â°F for 18 to 20 minutes. (Cookies should be soft in center when taken from oven.)
  6. Leave in pan and cut into 2-inch squares.
