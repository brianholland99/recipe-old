Chicken Broccoli Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 6 boneless chicken breast
  • 1 large package frozen broccoli
  • 2 can cream of chicken soup
  • 2 soup can milk
  • 6 slice American cheese
  • bread crumbs

Steps:

  1. Put broccoli in 9" x 13" baking dish.
  2. Slow brown chicken in Crisco oil about 20 minutes. Put chicken over broccoli.
  3. Mix soup and milk. Pour over chicken and broccoli.
  4. Lay 1 slice of cheese on each chicken. Sprinkle bread crumbs over all.
  5. Bake for 2 hours at 300Â°F
