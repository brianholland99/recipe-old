Chocolate Cheesecake

crust

    Ingredients:

    • 1 1/2 cup graham cracker crumbs
    • 1/3 cup melted butter
steps:
  1. Mix well, press firmly into bottom of 9" springform pan. Bake at 350°F for 6 minutes.

cheesecake

    Ingredients:

    • 1 1/3 cup melted semi-sweet chocolate morsels
    • 24 ounce softened cream cheese
    • 1 1/2 cup sugar
    • 1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
    • 3 eggs
    • 1 tablespoon cocoa
    • 8 ounce sour cream
steps:
  1. Beat cream cheese on high until fluffy. Add sugar and beat well.
  2. Add eggs one at a time. Add melted chocolate, cocoa, vanilla and mix well. Add sour cream. Pour into pan. Bake at 300°F for 1 hour and 40 minute.
  3. Let cool to room temp. Use knife to loosen sides. Chill for 8 hours.

whipped cream frosting

    Ingredients:

    • 1 1/2 cup whipping cream
    • 3 tablespoon cocoa
    • 2 tablespoon powdered sugar
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla
steps:
  1. Beat on high until stiff peaks form.
  2. Spread whipped cream topping on top of cake. Garnish as desired.
