Chocolate Easter Eggs

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup margarine
  • 1 pound confectioner's sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 3 ounce cream cheese
  • chocolate
  • peanut butter, coconut, or cherries/nuts (optional)

Steps:

  1. Mix softened margarine and cream cheese.
  2. Mix in vanilla and confectioner's sugar.
  3. If adding flavoring, add now to desired taste.
  4. Shape into eggs, place on wax paper and freeze overnight.
  5. Melt chocolate in a double boiler and dip eggs.
  6. Place back on wax paper and freeze until solid.
Source: Joyce Stevens
