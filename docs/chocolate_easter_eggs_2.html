Chocolate Easter Eggs 2

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup margarine
  • 2 pound confectioner's sugar
  • 8 ounce cream cheese
  • dark chocolate
  • Choose:
    • 2 cup peanut butter
      • Comment: Easy to roll due to oil in peanut butter.
      or
    • 2 cup coconut
      • or
    • 50 drop peppermint oil
      • or
    • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa
      • or
    • 2 teaspoon vanilla
      • or
    • 50 drops strawberry flavoring
    • 2 package unsweeted strawberry Kool-aid
      • or
    • 35 drops orange oil
    • 1 package unsweeted orange Kool-aid

Steps:

  1. Mix softened margarine and cream cheese.
  2. Mix in vanilla and confectioner's sugar.
  3. Add flavoring to desired taste.
  4. Shape into eggs, place on wax paper and freeze overnight.
  5. Melt chocolate in a double boiler and dip eggs.
  6. Place back on wax paper and freeze until solid.
Source: Modified from Chocolate Easter Eggs from Joyce Stevens
