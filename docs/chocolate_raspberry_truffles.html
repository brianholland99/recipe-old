Chocolate Raspberry Truffles

Ingredients:

  • 22 ounce bitter sweet chocolate
  • 6 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup seedless raspberry jam
  • Choose:
    • 2 tablespoon chambord
      • or
    • 2 tablespoon raspberry brandy

Steps:

  1. Combine 10 ounce chocolate and butter in a glass bowl.
  2. Beat 1 to 1 1/2 minutes on high.
  3. Stir in jam and chambord. Cover and refrigerate 4 hours.
  4. Scrape into 1 inch balls w/ melon baller. Put in freezer until firm.
  5. Place remaining 12 ounce of chocolate into microwave for 3 to 3 1/2 minutes or until melted. Dip truffles in chocolate. Place on paper lined baking pan.
  6. Refrigerate 1/2 hour. Put in air-tight container.
  7. Store in refrigerator 2 weeks or freezer for 1 month.
Serves
Source: Jackie Heath
