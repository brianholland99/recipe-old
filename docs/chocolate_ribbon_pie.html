Chocolate Ribbon Pie

Ingredients:

  • 4 ounce softened Philadelphia cream cheese
  • 2 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • 2 cup milk
  • 8 ounce Cool Whip
  • 1 9-inch chocolate-flavored crumb crust
  • 2 package Jello chocolate flavor instant pudding

Steps:

  1. Beat cream cheese, sugar, and 1 tablespoon milk in a large bowl until smooth. Gently stir in 1/2 of the whipped topping. Spread on bottom of crust.
  2. Pour 2 cups cool milk into large bowl. Add pudding mixes. Beat with wire wisk 2 minutes. (Mixture will be thick) Pour over cream cheese layer.
  3. Refrigerate 4 hours or until set. Just before serving, spread remaining whipped topping over pudding layer. Garnish with shaved chocolate.
Serves 8
