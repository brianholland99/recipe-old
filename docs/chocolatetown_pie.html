Chocolatetown Pie

Category: Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 9-inch unbaked pie shell
  • 1/2 cup softened butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 cup Hershey's semi-sweet chocolate chip
  • Choose:
    • 1 cup chopped walnut
      • or
    • 1 cup chopped pecan

Steps:

  1. Prepare pastry shell; set aside.
  2. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  3. In small mixer bowl, cream butter; add eggs and vanilla.
  4. Combine sugar and flour, add to creamed mixture.
  5. Stir in chocolate chips and nuts.
  6. Pour into unbaked pastry shell.
  7. Bake for 45 to 50 minutes or until golden brown.
  8. Cool about one hour, serve warm.
Serves
