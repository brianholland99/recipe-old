Cinnamon Streusel Cake

Category: Cake
cake

    Ingredients:

    • 1 box Duncan Hines yellow cake mix
    • 1 (4 serving size box) vanilla instant pudding
    • 2 tablespoon Crisco oil
    • 1 1/3 cup water
    • 2 egg

Streusel

    Ingredients:

    • 1/2 cup flour
    • 1/2 cup brown sugar
    • 2 teaspoon cinnamon
    • 2 tablespoon butter

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
  2. In a large bowl blend cake mix, pudding, oil, water and eggs. Beat 2 minutes.
  3. Spread 3/4 of butter evenly in greased and floured 10" tube pan.
  4. Combine streusel ingredients.
  5. Sprinkle 2/3 cups of mixture over batter.
  6. Then add rest of the batter and top with remaining streusel.
  7. Bake 40-50 min.
Comment: Altitude - Stir 1/4 cup flour into mix. Mix as directed using 1 1/2 cups water. Bake at 400 for 35-40 min.
