Coffee Cake

topping

    Ingredients:

    • 3 tablespoon flour
    • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
    • 1/4 cup sugar
    • 2 tablespoon butter

batter

    Ingredients:

    • 1 1/2 cup flour
    • 2 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • 3/4 cup sugar
    • 1/4 cup shortening
    • 1 egg
    • 1/2 cup milk

Steps:

  1. Blend all ingredients for topping with fork until well mixed and crumbly then set aside.
  2. Sift flour, baking powder, salt and sugar together in a bowl.
  3. Cut in shortening until mix is like coarse corn meal.
  4. Combine milk and egg then beat.
  5. Pour milk and egg mixture into flour mixture and stir until just mixed.
  6. Grease a 12"x8"x2" pan.
  7. Turn batter into pan and spread to corners.
  8. Sprinkle topping evenly over batter.
  9. Bake at 375Â°F for 15 to 20 minutes until golden brown.
Serves 8-12
