Cooked Icing

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoon flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 1/2 cup margarine
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 cup sugar

Steps:

  1. Mix flour and milk. Cook until it is thick and then put it in the refrigerator to cool.
  2. After the flour and milk are cool, add the rest of the ingredients.
  3. Beat until it looks like whipped cream.
