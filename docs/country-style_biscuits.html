Country-Style Biscuits

Ingredients:

  • 2 3/4 cup flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/3 cup shortening
  • 1 cup old mill buttermilk

Steps:

  1. Knead for 30 seconds.
  2. Cut 1/2" thick.
  3. Put on ungreased cokie sheet.
  4. Bake 10 to 15 minutes.
Comment: Temperature wasn't specified.
