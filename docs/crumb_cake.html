Crumb Cake

Category: Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup butter
  • 3 cup flour
  • 2 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 3 egg
  • 3 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 cup milk

Steps:

  1. Blend together butter, flour, sugar and salt.
  2. Reserve 1 cup of mixture to which is added cinnamon for topping.
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients to the main mixture
  4. Pour in pan and add topping.
  5. Bake @ 350Â°F for 40-45 minutes.
