cake

    Ingredients:

    • 3 cup Bisquick baking mix
    • 1/4 cup sugar
    • 1/4 cup melted margarine
    • 1/2 cup milk
    • 1/4 cup strawberry, apricot or raspberry preserves

Steps:

  1. Heat oven to 375°F.
  2. Mix baking mix, sugar, margarine and milk; beat vigorously 30 sec.
  3. Turn dough onto surface well dusted with baking mix.
  4. Knead 30 times. Pat dough evenly in bottom and up side of ungreased round pan 9x1 1/2".
  5. Prepare Cheese filling; pour over dough.
  6. Bake until edge is golden brown and filling is set, about 30 min.
  7. Cool 10 min. Spread with preserves. Serve warm.
Serves 8
Comment: High altitude: Heat oven to 400°F; Bake 30 to 35 min. Cheese filling may swirl through coffee cake during baking.
