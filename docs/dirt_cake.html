Dirt Cake

Category: Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup margarine
  • 8 ounce cream cheese
  • 2 large packages vanilla instant pudding
  • 3 1/2 cup milk
  • 16 ounce Cool Whip
  • 1 package Oreo

Steps:

  1. Mix margarine and cream cheese.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix pudding mix and milk.
  3. Mix both mixtures together. Fold in Cool Whip.
  4. Crumble the package of Oreo cookies.
  5. Alternate layes of Oreo crumbs and mixture in a deep dish. Start and end with Oreos.
