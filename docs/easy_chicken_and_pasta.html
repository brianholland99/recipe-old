Easy Chicken and Pasta

Category: Entree

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 1 can Campbell's Condensed Reduced Fat Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • 1 soup can water
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 cup frozen vegatable combination (brocolli, cauliflower,carrots
  • 2 cup uncooked medium egg noodles

Steps:

  1. In medium non-stick skillet over medium heat, cook chicken until browned. Set chicken aside
  2. Add soup, water, garlic powder and vegetable combination. Heat to a boil. Add noodles. Cook over medium heat 5 minutes, stirring often.
  3. Return chicken to pan. Cook 5 minutes or until noodles are done, stirring often.
Serves 4
Source: Campbell's
