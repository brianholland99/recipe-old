Easy Peanut Butter Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 14 ounce sweetened condensed milk
  • 3/4 cup peanut butter
  • 2 cup baking mix
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • sugar

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°.
  2. Combine condensed milk and peanut butter in mixer bowl; beat until smooth.
  3. Add baking mix and vanilla; mix well.
  4. Shape into 1-inch balls; roll in sugar.
  5. Place 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet; flatten with fork.
  6. Bake at 375°F for 6 to 8 minutes or until light brown; do not overbake.
  7. Cool on wire rack. Store, tightly covered, at room temperature.
Makes 60
