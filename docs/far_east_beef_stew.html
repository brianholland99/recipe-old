Far East Beef Stew

Ingredients:

  • 24 ounce Armour Star beef stew
  • 8 ounce drained and sliced water chestnut
  • 1/2 cup cut green onion
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • cooked rice
  • chow mein noodles

Steps:

  1. In fry pan, combine stew, water chestnuts, green onions, soy sauce and ginger.
  2. Simmer 10 minutes.
  3. Serve over rice and garnish with noodles.
Serves 4
