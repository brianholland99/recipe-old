Flo's Pumpkin Scones

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup mashed pumpkin
  • 2 cup self-raising flour

Steps:

  1. Put butter, salt and sugar in mix master, then add egg followed by mashed pumpkin.
  2. Finally add by hand sifted self-raising flour. Don't handle mix too much.
  3. Drop onto baking sheet.(~1/2 cup per scone)
  4. Cook in very hot oven 250°C/450°F for 15-20 minutes.
Comment: If using very dry pumpkin, may need 2 taplespoons milk.
Source: Paul "Chucky" Watson
