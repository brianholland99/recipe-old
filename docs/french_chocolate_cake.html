French Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • Choose:
    • 7 ounce dark baking chocolate
      • or
    • 7 ounce semi-sweet chocolate
  • 1 ounce dark baking chocolate
  • 1 pound butter
  • 5 egg
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/3 cup hazelnut (optional)
  • whipped cream (optional)
  • shaved chocolate (optional)

Steps:

  1. Grease a 9 1/2 inch springform pan.
  2. Preheat oven to 425Â°F.
  3. Melt chocolate and butter in thick pan over low heat.
  4. Beat eggs and sugar until airy.
  5. Carefully stir somewhat cooled chocolate mixture into egg mixture.
  6. Blend flour, nut, and baking powder into batter.
  7. Pour into pan.
  8. Bake for 17 minutes. Watch closely. This cake is best if center is not firm. Do not overbake!
  9. Top with whipped cream and shaved chocolate.
