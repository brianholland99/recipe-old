German Sweet Chocolate Brownies

Ingredients:

  • 4 ounce Baker's German's Sweet Baking Chocolate
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 cup chopped pecan
  • 1 1/3 cup Baker's angel flake coconut
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup milk

Steps:

  1. Heat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Microwave chocolate and butter in larger bowl on high 2 minutes or until butter is melted. Stir until chocolate is completely melted.
  3. Stir sugar into chocolate until well blended. Mix in eggs and vanilla. Stir in flour and 1/2 cup of the pecans until well blended. Spread in greased foil-lined 8-inch square pan.
  4. Mix coconut, remaining 1/2 cup pecans and brown sugar in same bowl. Stir in milk until well blended. Spoon mixture evenly over brownie batter.
  5. Bake 40 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out with fudgey crumbs. Do not overbake. Cut into squares.
Serves 16
Comment: Prep time: 15 min, Bake time: 55 min.
