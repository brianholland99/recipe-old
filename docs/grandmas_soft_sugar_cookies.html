Grandma's Soft Sugar Cookies

Category: Cookie

Ingredients:

  • 4 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 egg
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 3/4 cup sour cream

Steps:

  1. Heat oven to 450°F.
  2. Grease cookie sheet.
  3. Place cookies on sheet.
  4. Sprinkle with sugar.
  5. Bake 9-10 minutes @ 450°F.
Source: Doris Holland
