Green Bean Casserole

Ingredients:

  • Choose:
    • 32 ounce frozen green beans
      • or
    • 4 can - 5 can green beans
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 can french fried onion rings
  • 3 ounce real bacon bits
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper

Steps:

  1. Thaw and drain green beans
  2. Heat oven to 350Â°F.
  3. In bowl, combine beans, soup, milk, 1/2 can cnion rings, 1/2 of bacon bits, and pepper.
  4. Pour into 1 1/2 quart casserole.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes.
  6. Top with remaining onions and bacon bits. Bake 5 minutes longer.
