Homemade Salsa

Ingredients:

  • 3 - 4 mashed tomato
  • 4 ounce diced green chilies
  • 1/4 cup diced green pepper
  • 1/4 cup diced onion
  • 2 diced jalapeno
  • 1/2 teaspoon cilantro
  • 3 clove chopped garlic
  • 2 shake tabasco

Steps:

  1. Mix together and serve.
