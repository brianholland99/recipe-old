Jerry's Rice Pudding

Ingredients:

  • 2 quart milk
  • 1 pint half and half
  • 5 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup rice
  • 1 ounce pure vanilla
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 large box vanilla pudding
  • cinnamon

Steps:

  1. Put milk, half and half, sugar, rice, and vanilla all together.
  2. Cook over medium heat. Cook until rice done.
  3. Gradually add pudding until it thickens.
  4. Add well beaten eggs when mixture stops boiling; take off the stove to add eggs or it will curdle.
  5. Pour in bowl and add cinnamon.
Source: Carol Hahn
