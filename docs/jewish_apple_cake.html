Jewish Apple Cake

cake

    Ingredients:

    • 2 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
    • 3 cup flour
    • 4 egg
    • 2 cup sugar
    • 1/2 cup orange juice
    • 3 teaspoon baking powder
    • 1 cup oil

apple mixture

    Ingredients:

    • 5 sliced apple
    • 2 teaspoon cinnamon
    • 5 tablespoon sugar

Steps:

  1. Place 1/2 batter in pan.
  2. Then add 1/2 apple mixture to 1/2 cake.
  3. Add the remaining cake to the remaining apples.
  4. Bake at 350Â°F for 1 1/2 hours.
  5. Let stand for 1 hour before removing from pan.
