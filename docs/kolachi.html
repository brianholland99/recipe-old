Kolachi

dough

    Ingredients:

    • 2 cup flour
    • 1 package dry yeast
    • 1/2 cup oleo
    • 2 egg yolks
    • 1/2 cup sour cream
steps:
  1. Put flour in mixing bowl. Mix in yeast. Cut in oleo until crumbly.
  2. Add yolks and sour cream. Mix well and form into a ball. Knead on lightly floured board until smooth (5-10 times). Divide into 3 balls. Chill for one hour.

filling

    Ingredients:

    • 1 pound ground walnuts
    • 8 egg whites
    • 1 box chopped dates
    • 1/2 cup sugar
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla
steps:
  1. Beat egg whites untill stiff. Mix all ingredients into egg whites.
  2. Cook over low heat until hot. Stir. Do not boil! (Will burn easily)

Steps:

  1. Roll dough out thinly on board with powdered sugar. Spoon small line of filling at one end and roll until dough overlaps. Cut rolled part from the rest of the dough and place on greased cookie sheet. Repeat process until dough and/or filling is used up.
  2. Bake at 350Â°F for about 25 minutes or until browned.
