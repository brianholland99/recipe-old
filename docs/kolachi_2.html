Kolachi

dough

    Ingredients:

    • 16 ounce cream cheese
    • 1 pound margarine
    • 4 cup flour
    • 1 pinch salt
steps:
  1. Mix and cool in refrigerator.

filling

    Ingredients:

    • 1 pound ground walnuts
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla
    • 3/4 cup sugar
    • 1/2 cup half and half cream

Steps:

  1. Use confectioner's sugar ro roll out dough. Don't roll out all at once.
  2. Cur in pieces 2 inch by 2 1/2 inch. Drop filling by 1/2 teaspoon on cut pieces.
  3. Roll after spreading the filling.
  4. Place folded side down on greased cookie sheet.
  5. Bake at 350Â°F for 20-25 minutes.
