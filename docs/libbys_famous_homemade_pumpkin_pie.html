Libby's Famous Homemade Pumpkin Pie

Ingredients:

  • 2 slightly beaten egg
  • 16 ounce Libby's Solid Pack Pumpkin
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground clove
  • 12 ounce Carnation Evaporated Milk
  • 1 9-inch unbaked pie shell

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Combine filling ingredients in order given.
  3. Pour into pie shell.
  4. Bake 15 minutes. Reduce temperature to 350°F.
  5. Bake an additional 45 minutes or until knife inserted near center comes out clean.
Serves
