Mom's Blackberry Cake

Category: Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 4 tablespoon milk
  • 1 3/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 cup blackberries
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves (optional)
  • 1/2 cup coarsely chopped walnuts (optional)

Steps:

  1. Cream together butter and sugar. Beat in eggs.
  2. Dissolve baking soda in milk. Add to mixture.
  3. Stir in flour, salt, and vanila.
  4. Add in the optional ingredients if desired.
  5. Add in blackberries. Pour into cake pan.
  6. Bake at 350Â°F for 1/2 hour.
Comment: Add butter icing if desired.
