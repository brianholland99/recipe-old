No Bake Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 3 cup quick Oats
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter

Steps:

  1. Combine sugar, butter and milk. Boil for 2 minutes.
  2. Remove from heat and add vanilla and peanut butter.
  3. Stir to dissolve peanut butter.
  4. Add nuts and oatmeal.
  5. Drop by teaspoon on waxed paper.
