Peach-Pineapple Cobbler

Category: Dessert

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cup self-rising flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 cup plus 1 cup sugar
  • 1 can peach pie filling
  • 1 can crushed pineapple (16-ounce)
  • 1/2 cup butter

Steps:

  1. Mix together flour, salt, and 1 1/2 cup sugar.
  2. Mix in milk,
  3. Spoon in peach pie filling and crushed pineapple - juice included.
  4. Dot butter on top of mixture.
  5. Sprinkle with remaining cup of sugar.
  6. Bake at 325-350Â°F for 45-60 minutes.
