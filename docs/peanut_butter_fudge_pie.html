Peanut Butter Fudge Pie

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounce Cool Whip
  • 1 9-inch graham cracker crumb crust
  • 1/3 cup fudge sauce
  • 1 cup cold milk
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1 (3 1/2 ounce) package Vanilla Flavor Instant Pudding and Pie Filling

Steps:

  1. Spread 1 cup of the whipped topping in bottom of pie crust. Freeze 10 minutes.
  2. Carefully spread fudge sauce over whipped toppings.
  3. Stir milk gradually into peanut butter in large bowl until smooth.
  4. Add pudding mix. Beat with wire whisk 1 to 2 minutes.
  5. Gently stir in remaining whipped topping.
  6. Carefully spoon over fudge sauce.
  7. Freeze 4 hours or until firm. Let stand at room temperature 15 minutes or until pie can be cut easily. Store leftover pie in freezer.
