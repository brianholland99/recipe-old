Peanut Butter Pie

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounce softened cream cheese
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup confectioner's sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 8 ounce Cool Whip
  • 1 9-inch graham cracker crust
  • chocolate syrup

Steps:

  1. Beat cream cheese and peanut butter together.
  2. While beating add sugar and milk.
  3. After mixed add Cool Whip.
  4. Pour into crust. Freeze overnight.
  5. Serve frozen with chocolate syrup.
