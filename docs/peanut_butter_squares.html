Peanut Butter Squares

Category: Dessert
bottom

    Ingredients:

    • 1 cup crunchy peanut butter
    • 1 cup melted margarine
    • 1 1/4 cup graham cracker crumbs
    • 3 cup confectioners sugar
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
steps:
  1. Cream together and press into jelly roll pan.

topping

    Ingredients:

    • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
    • 1 tablespoon margarine
steps:
  1. Melt together in double boiler.
  2. Add a little water until creamy.
  3. Spread over peanut butter mixture.

Steps:

  1. Cut before it sets then refrigerate.
