Pie Crusts

Category: Pie

Ingredients:

  • 2 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2/3 cup baking shortening
  • 4 tablespoon - 6 tablespoon water

Steps:

  1. Mix flour and salt together.
  2. Cut in shortening.
  3. Add 4 tablespoon water.
  4. Mix together.
  5. If needed add more water. (Usally more than 4 is needed.)
Serves
