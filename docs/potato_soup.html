Potato Soup

Ingredients:

  • 6 slice bacon
  • 2 stalk chopped celery
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 21 ounce condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 26 ounce evaporated milk
  • 2 tablespoon parsley
  • 2 carrot
  • 2 teaspoon butter
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cup cubed potato
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (optional)

Steps:

  1. Brown bacon, then saute onions and celery in drippings.
  2. Add all ingredients to a crock pot and cook until well blended and creamy.
