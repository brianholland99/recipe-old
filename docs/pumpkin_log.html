Pumpkin Log

Category: Dessert
cake

    Ingredients:

    • 3 egg
    • 2/3 cup pumpkin
    • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
    • 1 cup sugar
    • 3/4 cup flour
    • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
    • 1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
steps:
  1. Mix cake ingredients, except for nuts, well.
  2. Line a 10x15 jelly roll pan with wax paper.
  3. Pour cake mixture into pan and spinkle with nuts.
  4. Bake at 375Â°F for 15 minutes.
  5. Turn onto a dish towel sprinkled with granulated sugar as soon as it comes out of the oven.
  6. Remove wax paper and roll immediately while hot.
  7. Cool completely in towel.

filling

    Ingredients:

    • 2 tablespoon softened butter
    • 8 ounce softened cream cheese
    • 3/4 teaspoon vanilla
    • 1 cup confectioner's sugar
steps:
  1. Beat filling well with electric mixer.

Steps:

  1. Unroll cake and fill with cream cheese mixture.
  2. Roll like a jelly roll and keep refrigerated.
