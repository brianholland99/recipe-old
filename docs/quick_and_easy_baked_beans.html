Quick and Easy Baked Beans

Ingredients:

  • 3 cans pork and beans
  • 1/2 cup catsup
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon prepared mustard
  • 3/4 pound - 1 pound ground beef
  • 4 slice - 6 slice bacon

Steps:

  1. Chop bacon strips and saute with ground beef. Drain.
  2. Add to other ingredients.
  3. Bake at 350Â°F for 35 to 45 minutes in 9"x9" baking dish or cook in crock pot.
