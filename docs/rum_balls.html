Rum Balls

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup crushed vanilla wafers
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 1/2 cup ground walnuts
  • 2 tablespoon cocoa
  • 2 tablespoon light corn syrup
  • Choose:
    • 1/4 cup rum
      • or
    • 1/4 cup whiskey
      • or
    • 1/4 cup brandy

Steps:

  1. Mix together.
  2. Roll in powdered sugar.
