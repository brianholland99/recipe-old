Scalloped Pineapple

Ingredients:

  • 3 well-beaten egg
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup margarine
  • 4 slice bread
  • 1 20-ounce can crushed pineapple

Steps:

  1. Break bread into small pieces
  2. Beat eggs and add other ingredients including juice from pineapple
  3. Bake at 350Â°F for 45 minutes.
