Soft Pretzels

Ingredients:

  • 1 envelope dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 cup - 5 cup flour
  • 1 beaten egg
  • course salt

Steps:

  1. Dissolve yeast in warm water. Add sugar, salt, and flour.
  2. Turn dough onto lightly floured board. Knead until smooth (5 minutes).
  3. Cut off small pieces of dough. Roll into ropes and twist into traditional pretzel shape.
  4. Arrange on greased cookie sheets. Brush with egg. Sprinkle with course salt.
  5. Bake at 425Â°F for 12 to 15 minutes or until brown.
  6. Serve within a few days.
