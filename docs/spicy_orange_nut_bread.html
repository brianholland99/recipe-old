Spicy Orange Nut Bread

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup ground orange rind
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/8 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 3 tablespoon oil
  • 2 unbeaten egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon orange extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 drop almond extract
  • 2 drop lemon extract
  • 3/4 cup coarsely chopped walnuts

Steps:

  1. In advance, grind or chop in blender enough orange rind (about 2 to 3 medium sized oranges) to measure 1 cup.
  2. Add water and sugar, heat to boiling, then cover and cook over low heat 10 to 15 minutes, until rind is tender and practically all of the water is absorbed.
  3. Cool. Sift dry ingredients into mixing bowl.
  4. Add milk, oil or shortening, eggs, and flavorings.
  5. Beat at low speed just until well mixed.
  6. Mix in cooled orange peel and walnuts.
  7. Divide the soft batter evenly in two greased 8x4x2 1/2-inch loaf pans, or use one 9x5x3-inch pan.
  8. Sprinkle tops generously with a mixture of 1/2 tsp cinnamon and 2 tbsp sugar.
  9. Bake at 350Â°F, allowing 45 to 50 minutes for smaller pan, 55 to 60 minutes for one larger pan, until well browned and done when tested.
  10. Turn out and cool on rack.
Comment: For breakfast or coffee break or with luncheon fruit salad, serve this fragrant bread plain with butter, or waffle toasted. To "waffle" it, toast unbuttered half-inch slices in a hot waffle baker.
