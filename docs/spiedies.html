Spiedies

Ingredients:

  • 1/3 cup oil
  • 1/3 cup vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon paparika
  • 1 tablespoon parsley
  • 1 tablespoon oregano
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon basil leaves
  • 1 teaspoon celery salt
  • 1 dash rosemary
  • garlic
  • 1 chopped small onion
  • Choose:
    • 1 pound cubed chicken breast
      • or
    • 1 pound cubed pork
      • or
    • 1 pound cubed beef

Steps:

  1. Coat meat with oil.
  2. Add other ingredients.
  3. Mix and marinate for 2 to 3 days.
  4. Place meat on skewers.
  5. Grill until cooked.
Source: Doris Holland
