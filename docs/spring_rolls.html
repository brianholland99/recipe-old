Spring Rolls

Category: Appetizer

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 pound bean sprouts
  • 1/4 pound shrimp
  • 1/2 pound pork
  • 6 scallion
  • 3 tablespoon oil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 20 egg roll wrappers

Steps:

  1. Wash bean sprouts and pick them over. Shell, devein and dice shrimp. Dice the pork fine and cut the scallions into one inch lengths.
  2. Heat a pan with oil and when hot, saute the pork and the shrimp for one minute. Add the scallions and bean sprouts and stir for another two minutes. Add the sugar and salt and stir once or twice. Remove from heat.
  3. Place two teaspoons of the filling on each wrapping and fold it into a long package like a roll. Wet ends with water and close securely. Heat pan and fill up to one inch of oil. Deep fry the rolls for six minutes or until they turn golden brown. Serve very hot with soy sauce.
