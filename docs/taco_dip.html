Taco Dip

Ingredients:

  • 16 ounce sour cream
  • 8 ounce cream cheese
  • 8 ounce taco sauce
  • 1 package taco seasoning
  • 8 ounce grated sharp cheese
  • 1 diced pepper
  • 1 diced tomato
  • chopped black olives
  • 1/2 head lettuce

Steps:

  1. Mix sour cream and cream cheese together.
  2. Add taco seasoning and beat well.
  3. Put dip in bowl, Layer vegetables over dip. Top with cheese. Pour sauce over cheese.
