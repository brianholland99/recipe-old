Toll House Pie

Category: Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 9-inch unbaked pie shell*
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
  • 1 cup melted and cooled to room temperature butter
  • 1 cup Nestle Toll House semi-sweet chocolate morsels
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F.
  2. In large bowl, beat eggs until foamy.
  3. Add flour, sugar and brown sugar; beat until well blended.
  4. Blend in melted butter.
  5. Stir in Nestle Toll House semi-sweet chocolate morsels and walnuts.
  6. Pour into pie shell.*
  7. Bake at 325°F for 1 hour.
  8. Serve warm with whipped cream or ice cream, if desired.
Serves
Comment: If using frozen pie shell, it's necessary to use deep dish style, thawed. Place on cookie sheet and bake additional 10 minutes.
