Vegetable Lasagne

Ingredients:

  • 9 lasagne nodles
  • 1 chopped medium onion
  • 1 sliced zucchini
  • 2 cup sliced and steamed broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup - 3/4 cup grated carrot
  • 1/2 package thawed and drained frozen spinach
  • 1 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 15 ounce ricotta cheese
  • 2 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 pound grated mozzarella cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F
  2. Saute chopped onions and zucchini in 3 tablespoons of olive oil until tender.
  3. Beat eggs; add to ricotta cheese.
  4. Add basil, parmesan cheese, and salt to egg-ricotta mixture.
  5. Spread 1/2 cup spaghetti sauce into bottom of 9x13 inch pan.
  6. Place one layer of noodles on sauce; spread all of the ricotta mixture onto the noodles.
  7. Place another layer of noodles atop the ricotta mixture; pour 2 cups of sauce onto noodles; arrange all of the vegetables on top of sauce.
  8. Add another layer of noodles; pour the remaining sauce over the final layer of noodles and top with mozzarella cheese.
  9. Bake 45 to 60 minutes. Let cool 10 minutes before serving.
